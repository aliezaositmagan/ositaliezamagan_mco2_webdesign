SELF INTRODUCTION VLOG

MY REFLECTION

Creating my self-introduction vlog for our Web Design course gave me the chance to share who I am and what I can do. And in this activity, I talked about my background, my skills, and the things I know about web designing. Through this vlog, I realized that web design is not only about writing code—it is also about expressing creativity and communicating clearly through a website’s appearance and content. I enjoyed explaining my personal interests and how they connect to web development.
