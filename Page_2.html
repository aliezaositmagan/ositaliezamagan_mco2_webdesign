HOUSEHOLD WASTE MANAGEMENT OF BARANGAY CABATUAN OQUENDO DISTRICT, CALBAYOG CITY

CHAPTER 1

THE PROBLEM AND ITS SETTING

Introduction

A healthy sanitary enviroment means a highly intellectual and fully aware community people. Man always strives to live in a place where nature bountifully abounds, everything is in order and everybody desires to cross fingers towards a worthwile end. Environmental protection is everyone's concern. As residents of the planet earth, it is one of our responsibilities to keep enviroment healthy and worth living and preserve it for the generations ahead of us.

Waste management is a system of handling wastes that is environmentally sound. This system requires information dissemination to the households because some people don't know how to deal with their wastes.

Among the problems, it is the waste disposal that involves most people. There are households that would take "short cuts" in disposing garbage and there are those that would comply according to the prescribed methods.

Man throw away almost everywhere, neglecting the fact that dumped trashes and garbage results in unsanitary surroundings that eventually results in disease due to carrying insects and canines.

Environment sanitation is not new in the Philippines. Government efforts were already directed towards the protection and promotion of health considered of paramount importance. As a result, Presidential Decree No. 856 otherwise known as "Sanitation Code of the Philippines" was passed on Dec. 1975 and was made part of the law of the land. Paragraph (c), Section 83 of the Decree states that refuse shall not be throwing in any street, sidewalk, yard, and park or in a body of water. It shall be stored in a suitable container while awaiting its final disposal.

SIGNIFICANCE OF THE PROBLEM

The results of this study shall benifit the following groups of people.

Barangay Officials. As a primary official responsible in the implementation of Solid Waste Management Program in the Barangay, they can redefine and formulate the necessary measures in giving solutions to problem.

City Government of Calbayog. To motivate sustained efforts of participation and initiatives, the city government through its policy makers can formulate and implement laws, rules and regulations and can redefine pass appropriate strategies management. on household waste management.

Barangay Folks of Barangay Cabatuan. The results of the study will give awareness to the barangay folks about health, environment and sustainable development issues and why they should focus their behavior toward waste management.

Researcher. This will provide needed data and relevant information for further research undertaking.
