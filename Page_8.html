MY REFLECTION

During Exercise 8, creating a demo vlog about float, overflow, height, width, background images, and position helped me clearly see how each CSS property affects the layout of a webpage in real time. As I recorded the demo, I became more aware of how small adjustments—like changing an element’s width or applying a float—can completely shift the structure and appearance of a design. Working with overflow and background images also taught me the importance of controlling how content behaves inside its container, especially when elements grow or overlap. Overall, the exercise strengthened my understanding of layout fundamentals and gave me more confidence in explaining and applying these CSS concepts
